Egg Salad Sandwiches

When your kids have been misbehaving.

Honestly, who would make this? You could have had ham and cheese or peanut butter and jelly. But noooo, instead you're putting breakfast between bread.

It's actually pretty good. However, when you tell your 8 year old that there are eggs in his sandwich, he will immediately ask what he did wrong and why you're such a mean parent. You've been warned.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Condiments
  • Green Onion
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil, peel, and chop the eggs.
  2. Combine the chopped eggs and the remaining ingredients.
  3. Serve on bread or with crackers.
