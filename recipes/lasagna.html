Lasagna

A classic favorite of all pasta lovers.

Tell me, what's better than a cat? What's better cat is there than Garfield? What better food is there than Garfield's favorite food? That's right! Lasagna.

This dish is so full of seed oils, it will trick you into eating every last bit of it regardless of the calorie content. The meat is completely optional because, as you know, the purpose of lasagna is only to carb overload before the big sporting competition tomorrow.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion or Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Eat all of it before Jon notices.
